Modern Work @Build | February 10, 2023 Update

 

We’re very excited to send out this early report on Build 2023!

 

After three consecutive virtual Builds, we’ll be live and in-person at the beautiful, newly-built Seattle Convention Center – Summit Building. Build 2020 pivoted quickly to deliver the first virtual event at that scale and made history, reaching over 200,000 digital attendees. This year we will continue to build on this legacy with a robust digital venue for our global audience to complement the marquee event in Seattle.

 

We wanted to communicate early with what we know. Rest assured, there is still a lot of information to come as decisions are made. Please don’t hesitate to reach out with any questions.  

 

 

 

Microsoft Build 2023 will take place on May 23-25, 2023 at the Summit Building, Seattle Conference Center for the in-person experience complemented by a full Global Digital Engagement event. We are targeting 3,000 attendees for the in-person developer audience and the digital venue should attract over 200,000.

 

Pre-day

On May 22, pre-day workshops will be held for an additional cost of $225 per attendee, with a cap of 800 passes. Pre-day will consist of 1-hour workshops with approximately 100 attendees in each workshop. These sessions will feature technical content but will not overlap with core event sessions.

 

Attendee Celebration

Current plan is to announce an attendee celebration with food & beverage, music, and entertainment on Day 2, May 24. More details to come!

 

Content

Build 2023 content will be organized into three core themes, but content will align to the list of nine topics below that will feature on the website experience. As the core team is still in the early stages of content development, these are subject to change.

Breakout sessions will be in-person (45 minutes) and streamed to the digital venue. There will be a 30-minute break in between for room transition and audience Q&A. We’ll be reaching out for topic submissions.

On-demand sessions will be pre-recorded and discoverable in the session catalog.

Lab content is to be determined, with a call for content still to come. Labs are instructor-led, in-person sessions with real-time proctors.

 

Connection Zone

Piloted at Ignite, Meet-Ups will once again feature at Build. These are engaging experiences for attendees to talk with our experts on the show floor. We had a lot of learnings from Ignite that will be considered for this workstream. Details to come.

 

The opportunity to Demo our products and solutions in a one-to-few setting will be a great way to showcase technical content the developer audience. The modality of this workstream has not been finalized – we will share once we have more information.

 

Additional opportunities for attendee engagements are in discussion – stay tuned!

 

Next Steps

Planning V-Team with key contributors and stakeholders delivering content will kick-off on Feb 15. If you have key people who should join, please inform Paulette Lee.
